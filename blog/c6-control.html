Control

"What lies in our power to do, lies in our power not to do." — Aristotle

Sunday, 24 April 2016

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

Know when you are not in pain. I think this is a particularly important concept in the chapter. We are are so focussed on pain when it happens and yet when we aren't in pain, we don't stop to appreciate it. By stopping and acknowledging our lack of pain we can be happier every day.

Another thing I picked up from this chapter was the importance of not feeding 'monsters'. I have a bad habit of re-telling stories to myself over and over. I also like the idea of being comfortable and familiar with our emotions instead of willing them to disappear or giving in to them.

 

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

I'm really enjoying my mindfulness practice. I seriously feel like it's the reason why I'm not as stressed as I usually am. I still need to use my apps to guide me through meditation though.
