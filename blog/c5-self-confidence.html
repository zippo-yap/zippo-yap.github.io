Self Confidence

“The moment you doubt whether you can fly, you cease for ever to be able to do it.” ― J.M. Barrie, Peter Pan

Tuesday, 12 April 2016

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfulness and self awareness are essentially the same thing and therefore, because mindfulness is trainable, then self-awareness must also be trainable in similar ways.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I've been using the body scan technique for about 2 weeks now and I'm really enjoying it. I managed to practice meditating for just under 20 minutes today and boom - productiveness! Although I think part of this is also having a coffee right before I start meditating. I feel a lot calmer afterwards and I'm finding that it really helps me clear my mind before I start my day.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

We have the mindset that we are our emotions, however, by being mindful we can shift our mindset and realise that our emotions are something our body experiences. These physiological changes are something we can control/ignore with some practice. Emotions will come and go - they do not make you who you are.
